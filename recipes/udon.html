Udon

Description

Udon soup is a simple recipe of thick rice noodles, vegetable or bone broth, and assorted vegetables with some dishes including tofu or fried pork

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Thick Rice noodles
  • 12 fl oz Vegetable or Bone Broth
  • 2 oz Carrots
  • 2 oz Seaweed
  • 2 oz Broccoli
  • (Optional) 4 oz Tofu or Fried Pork

Steps

  1. Boil 12 fl oz of Veg or Bone Broth
  2. Add 8 oz Thick Rice Noodles, allowing noodles to cook in broth for 8-12 minutes depending on desired cook of noodle
  3. Add 2 oz Carrots, Seaweed, and Broccoli to the boiled broth
  4. (Optional) Add 4 oz Tofu or Fried Pork to broth
